Hackerspaces is a tool to help you discover and track hackerspaces around the world.

Know at a glance when your favorite hackerspace opens. And discover new hackerspaces around you using the map.

The search function allows you to quickly add hackerspaces to your favorite list and check their status!

- Know your favorite's hackerspace status anytime.
- Discover nearby hackerspaces using the map view.
- Search for any hackerspace in the world and get a look at their status.

If you want to know more about hackerspaces, please visit http://hackerspaces.org

Powered by OpenSpaceDirectory https://github.com/fixme-lausanne/OpenSpaceDirectory
